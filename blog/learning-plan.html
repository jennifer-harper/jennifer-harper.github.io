Learning plan Bootcamp

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

I am hoping to use the skills I gain on this course to get employed in a challenging, interesting job. I'd like to make an impact on my personal life. Getting a job with a salary rather than being a poor student would be a great start. I don't have a particular destination in mind but I would like to work for a company where I am generally excited and interested in what they do, their values or their goals.

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

I think my biggest limitation will be self confidence in my abilities. I don't like being watched when I do something, so I imagine peer programming will take some getting used to. But my strength in focus and determination will also help me get over this, as I'm excited to learn and become a better developer. I think my strengths and values are still the same. Everything is a work in process though, we learn and adapt to new situations.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

As mentioned previously, I don't like speaking in front of others, or not feeling confident in my knowledge. I've also noticed that I'm not as good as I thought I would be at taking breaks and asking for help, so I'm conscious of trying to be better at those. It's always hard to be kind to yourself in hard/stressful situations, so it's important to continue trying to do that during this time.

What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I'd like to develop a range of human skills, especially presentation skills and team work skills. I'd also like to become more patient and less stressed, maybe a better listener.

What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

I love the idea of having a team of people on hand to help me work through problems and teach me new skills. I know that this was available to me during foundations but I much prefer learning/ receiving help with a face to face element. It's much easier to point at a screen or explain something you're not sure how to explain when you don't have to type it or switch between video chat and share screen! But also the same as last time, I expect a team of people to support me and help me learn

What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp

My expectations of myself on Bootcamp are to use my time to learn as much as possible and have fun while doing it. I'd like to make some great friendships during this time. I think it's important to remain respectful and patient. Clear communication is essential. It is also important to look after yourself, stress and unhappiness can make you do things you regret. So maintaining work/life balance is as important as ever. I will continue to practice mindfulness, exercise, do other creative things and have time with friends and family where I can.
